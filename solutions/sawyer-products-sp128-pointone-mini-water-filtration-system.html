Solution Overview & Benefits

The Sawyer MINI Water Filter is a handheld water filtration device that is rated to 0.1 micron absolute, weighs only two ounces, and filters up to 100,000 gallons of water.

The MINI can be attached to a collapsible drinking pouch (included in product packaging), a hydration pack, a standard soda bottle, or it may also simply be used with a drinking straw to drink directly from the water source.
