Solution Overview & Benefits

The SunRay Power Panel is Nokero's pocket-sized solar charger. It is capable of charging mobile phone batteries and other electronic devices. With the SunRay, one can simply attach a device to the solar panel, place it in the sun, and charge. The SunRay is a low-cost option for users who have charging needs in off-grid locations.

The SunRay Power Panel uses new technology to deliver 30% more energy than typical panels.

The Power Panel's USB port is compatible with many devices, and comes with adaptors for Sony, Nokia, and Samsung phones. With the universally available USB cable, the charger can adapt to many other small-voltage devices.
