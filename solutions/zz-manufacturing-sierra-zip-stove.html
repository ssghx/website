Solution Overview & Benefits

The Sierra Zip Stove is an efficient portable stove that burns small pieces of wood, fuel pellets, or other organic carbon-based fuels using a battery-powered ventilation which feeds oxygen to the fire. Appropriate for camping or nomadic travel.

History & Development

Available on the market for the last decade, now available in titanium. Available for online purchase. Available in bulk order, delays in supply may occur.
