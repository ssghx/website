Proximity Designs Pressure Pumps

Solution Overview & Benefits

Proximity Designs' Pressure Pump systems include foot-powered pumps that draw water directly from wells onto fields for irrigation. All of the Original Pressure Pump models are locally manufactured.

The three Pressure Pump models include:

The Original

  • Suction Depth: 20 Feet
  • Price: $40 USD

The Red Rhino

  • Suction Depth: 25 Feet
  • Price: $36 USD

The Baby Buffalo

  • Suction Depth: 25 Feet
  • Price: $34 USD

History & Development

Established by Jim Taylor and Debbie Aung Din, Proximity Designs began in 2004, as a country office of iDE. Back then, Proximity Designs entered the Myanmar rural market with two treadle pumps, 13 staff and a distribution reach of just 600 villages. Now, eight years later, Proximity is accessible to nearly 80% of the rural population, employing a nationwide staff of over 350, and offering a complete range of services that address many of the most pressing needs of rural families.
