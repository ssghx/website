Solution Overview & Benefits

5 Star Stoves provide one and two-plate gasification stoves. The stoves support pots with a 10-liter capacity, and operate using biomass pellets. Each stove costs USD $41.

Working with local partners, 5 Star Stoves implements integrated cooking solutions. They provide assistance to small communities to create their own sustainable cookstove supply companies.
