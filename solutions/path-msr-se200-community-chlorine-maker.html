Solution Overview & Benefits

The MSR SE200 produces a chlorine solution for treating drinking water with salt, water, and AC or DC power.

The SE200 is easy to use and automatically produces a consistent concentration of chlorine to simplify dosing. Producing chlorine on-site with locally available inputs avoids the cost and supply chain issues associated with standard treatment methods.

History & Development

The SE200 was co-developed by PATH and MSR Global Health. It is currently available for purchase directly from MSR and will be made available through local distributors in East Africa.
