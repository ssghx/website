Solution Overview & Benefits

The Freeplay Energy Centre is a compact and versatile solar powered energy system. By harnessing and converting the sun's energy, it provides power to various lighting products, mobile phones, a water purifier and many other devices. the starter kit is provided with a 4 watt solar panel, power pack with controller and two bright LED bulbs and connecting cables.
