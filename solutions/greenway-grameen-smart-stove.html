Solution Overview & Benefits

The Greenway Smart Stove is a single burner cookstove designed for long-term, everyday use. The stove can burn any type of solid biomass fuel including, but not limited to, wood, cow dung and agricultural waste.

The patent-pending design produces 70% less smoke and uses 65% less fuel than traditional mud cookstoves. It adds convenience to users’ lives by reducing the time and/or money they must spend collecting or buying fuel and allowing for a more comfortable cooking experience.

History & Development

The Greenway Smart Stove has been tested and certified by the Ministry of New & Renewable Energy (MNRE), Government of India.
