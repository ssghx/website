Solution Overview & Benefits

Kyoto Butterfly uses Kyoto Mosaic mirror tiles to create multifunctional solar energy systems that allow the co-generation of electricity and heat. The Butterfly uses 20 times concentration, which requires only 5% the area of solar cells to generate the same power output.

The tracking system increases power output by 40% over fixed mounted systems. The resulting heat can be used for a number of vital applications, such as desalination, water heating, food drying and so on.

Features:

  • 4x1 meters
  • Lightweight - 6kg/m2
  • Aluminum and plastic
  • Automatic 2-axis solar tracking
  • Ground screw mounting

Outputs:

  • Electricity 800W
  • Hot Water 70°C
  • Superheated air 400°C
  • Storage for 24/7 operation
