Solution Overview & Benefits

The Grifaid Family Aquafilter速 is a household water filter inspired by public water facilities technology, using hollow fibres/ultra-filtration membranes. It removes all biological pathogens down to 0.01 microns. The Grifaid Family Aquafilter速 is designed to last a minimum of 5 years, requiring no spare parts, cartridges, or electricity. The Grifaid Family Aquafilter速 produces safe drinking water on demand, at a rate of 90 liters per hour.
