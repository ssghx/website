Solution Overview & Benefits

Insta Products manufactures low-cost flour based nutritional products for use in cases of acute and severe malnutrition. The fortified and enriched foods are pre cooked and come in a ready-to-eat form.

History & Development

Insta Products was launched in 2003 and received the GAIN Acumen Portfolio Fund in 2008. Insta Products are recognized UNICEF as a global supplier and are in collaboration with UNICEF, USAID, WFP, and other humanitarian organizations.

In addition, Insta is targeting consumers through traditional market channels including, kiosks, shops, and supermarkets.
