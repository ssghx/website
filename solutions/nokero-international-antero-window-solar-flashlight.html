Solution Overview & Benefits

The Nokero window solar flashlight is an essential component of any emergency preparedness plan. It attaches to a window with a suction cup, the solar panel facing out toward the sun, charging as it hangs. It provides up to 8 hours of runtime when fully charged.

Tech specs:

  • Light-weight and portable
  • Easy on/off slide-switch
  • Durable and UV-resistant ABS plastic
  • Attaches to a window or sits outdoors for charging
  • High quality amorphous solar panel
  • Ultra-efficient LED created specifically for long-runtime applications
  • Standard AA-sized NiMH battery that last for years
  • Brightness: 16 lumens
  • Run time: Up to 8 hours
  • Peak power watts Pmax (watts): 0.07watts
  • Maximum Power Voltage Vmax (V): 1.8V
  • Maximum Power Current IMMP (A): 40mA
  • Open Circuit Voltage Voc (V): 2.1V
  • Short Circuit Current ISC (A): 48mA
  • Operating Temperature: Max of battery is 50C
  • Storage Temperature: -20℃ to 40℃
  • No. of LED’s Used: 3
  • Watts / LED: 0.07W
  • LED Efficiency (lumens / watt): 78LM/W
  • Expected Life: 20,000 hours
