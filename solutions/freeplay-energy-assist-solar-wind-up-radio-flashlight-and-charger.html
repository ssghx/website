Solution Overview & Benefits

Designed specifically for harsh, off-grid environments Assist is a compact lightweight multi-band radio, flashlight and mobile phone charge with built-in siren. This radio is ideal for rapid deployment with comparatively low distribuiton costs

History & Development

Assist is used by organisations and governments for emergency preparation stockpiles and for distribution into refugee camps.
