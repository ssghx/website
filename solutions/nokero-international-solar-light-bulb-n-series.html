Nokero Solar Light Bulb N-Series

Solution Overview & Benefits

Nokero makes solar lights and chargers to meet the needs and budgets of communities living without access to electricity. They design products that are practical, intuitive, and cost-effective, and then partner with local organizations to get them to the communities who need them most.

The N-Series has five solar light bulb options: N182 - Start ($8.00) N200 - Crestone ($15.00) N210C - Crestone Pro ($20.00) N220 - Shavano ($25.00) N222- Huron ($45.00)

History & Development

The N-Series solar light bulbs capture sunlight and converts it into electricity. LED lights are powered by the reusable battery enclosed in the housing of the bulb.
