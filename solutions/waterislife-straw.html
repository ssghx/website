Solution Overview & Benefits

The WATERisLIFE Straw is a small, portable filtration device that provides pure, clean drinking water whenever it is immersed into a water source (just like a normal straw).

When used, the WATERisLIFE Straw filters out waterborne diseases which account for over 6,500 deaths every day – 5,000 of those being children. The Straw is proven to be effective against waterborne diseases such as typhoid, cholera, dysentery, guinea worm and diarrhea, removing particles as small as fifteen microns.

Each straw is sponsored at $10 per unit.

History & Development

WATERisLIFE is dedicated to making an impact and saving lives all over the world utilizing an integrated program to transform entire villages:

1.Distribute WATERisLIFE personal straw and point of use filters to villages during and after disaster situations to provide an immediate clean water solution to save lives.

  1. Create community Water, Sanitation and Hygiene (WASH) Action Groups to collaborate on the development of self-sustaining, long-term WASH programs in communities, schools, medical clinics and orphanages.
