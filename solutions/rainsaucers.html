RainSaucers

Solution Overview & Benefits

The RainSaucers™ system is a rain barrel accessory that allows users to harvest rainwater anywhere. The RainSaucer installs in minutes and cleanly collects fresh rainwater. It can be used for sustainable gardening, for small scaling farming, and for collecting clean drinking water.

Rather than using electricity to pump water, RainSaucers™ harvest rainwater where it is needed, no pumping required. In addition, the design of the RainSaucer™ ensures that the collected water is not exposed to contaminated surfaces, which can render water unsafe for consumption. The RainSaucers™ system also acts as an on-site capacitor. When it rains, crops are irrigated naturally while the RainSaucers™ system gets topped off. When there is no rain, farmers can access on-site reserves.

There are three sizes available: 45 square feet, 18 square feet, and 11.25 square feet. RainSaucers come with a 1 year limited warranty against defects.

History & Development

The RainSaucers™ system is a product of RainSaucers Inc., a startup based in the San Francisco Bay Area. Their mission is to create high quality, simple, and adorable RWH solutions.
