Nokia 105

Solution Overview & Benefits

The Nokia 105 is an inexpensive and durable cell phone, available worldwide. Retailing at approx. $20 per-unit, the Nokia 105 features a long-lasting battery allowing for up to 12.5 hours of talking time and 35 days on standby. Its rubber casing offers dust and splash protection, and additional features include FM radio, flashlight and 8MB of memory.
