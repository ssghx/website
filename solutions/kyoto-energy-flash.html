Solution Overview & Benefits

The Kyoto Flash is designed to replace dangerous, filthy and expansive kerosene lights and provide task light for studying, crafts, trade and security.

The Kyoto Flash has a solar panel that charges in the sunlight. It can be clipped to a belt so it can charge while users go about their days. A full charge provides 6 hours of light.

Features:

  • 9 unbreakable LED lights
  • 3 AA batters that last 2 years
  • shockproof
  • waterproof
  • two settings for either task or room light

Each Kyoto Flash comes with a 2 year warranty.
