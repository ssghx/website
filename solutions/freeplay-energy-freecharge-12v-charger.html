Solution Overview & Benefits

Mobile phones, GPS, flashlights and lanterns need never fail when most needed. This portable, lightweight energy source for personal electronic products ensures that a recharge is never more than a wind away. Powered entirely by human energy, FreeCharge powers any device for which the user has access to a cigarette lighter adaptor. FreeCharge charges all mobile phones and personal electronic devices (iPod, GPS, PDA etc.) from obsolete older models to those yet to be released.
