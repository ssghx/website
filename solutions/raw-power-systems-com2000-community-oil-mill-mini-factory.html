Solution Overview & Benefits

The COM2000 Community Oil Mill Mini-Factory has been designed to provide a sustainable business for a local community. Local collectors can harvest the seeds or nuts for processing into oil at the COM2000. The COM2000 is assembled and delivered in a new standard ISO 20 foot shipping container which becomes the on-site factory. The oil can be used for (i) power generation with an RPS2000 enabled generator, (ii) cooking, and (iii) soap making (see SMF2000 Soap Mini-Factory).

History & Development

The Community Oil Mill Mini-Factory was developed primarily to support Rural Electrification Programs. It can be deployed in areas where plant oil crops grow wild or are in cultivated plantations.
