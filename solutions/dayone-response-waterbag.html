Solution Overview & Benefits

The DayOne Waterbag is personal water treatment gear that provides all four elements of municipal water supply: collection, transport, treatment, and protected storage.

Product Details:

  • Ship 3x more Waterbags than collapsible containers.
  • 10 Liter capacity, compact and reusable.
  • Visual indication that water is clean.
  • Roll down sealing closure ensures safety from recontamination.
  • Clasped wide mouth opening for easy filling in only 3- inches of standing water.
  • Backpack straps decrease fatigue in hauling water long distances.
  • Meets WHO, Sphere, and US EPA emergency drinking water guidelines.
  • Multi-treatment processes flocculent, disinfectant and microfiltration.
  • Drinking hose for ease of dispensing.

History & Development

DayOne Response, Inc. developed and supplies the DayOne Waterbag™ addressing the vital need for clean water in disaster relief.
