Solution Overview & Benefits

Encore Player is an MP3 enables multi-band radio designed and engineered for the humanitarian sector. This innovative tool combines radio, media player and dedicated mobile phone charger. The user can record from voice, radio broadcasts or directly onto SD card meaning adults and children do not miss out on vital information.

History & Development

Encore Player is currently being distributed into global education, health , community listening and agricultural programs
