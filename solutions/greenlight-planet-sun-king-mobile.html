Solution Overview & Benefits

The Sun King Mobile is an affordable solar-powered light and mobile phone charger. The portable unit includes a 5.5 volt USB charging port and an LED light that emits 8X the brightness of a typical kerosene lamp.

Charged via a detachable 1.5 watt solar panel, the Sun King Mobile provides up to 36 hours of light on a single day's charge. A real time LED meter displays the amount of battery power remaining, and each battery comes with a five year warrenty. Mobile phone charging may take place at day or night, and several phone adapters are included for compatibility.

History & Development

In low-resource rural settings, villagers often walk 3 kilometers or more just to charge their mobile phone, costing them money and productivity due to lost working hours. The Sun King Mobile was developed to allow affordable and sustainable lighting and mobile charging in rural homes.
