Solution Overview & Benefits

Connexions is a content management system designed for learners and educators to store educational materials. It's content is available free to anyone over the internet. The materials can be accessed in a variety of digital formats and printed copies can be ordered. The system's content is highly modular to allow teachers to customize their curriculum. Independent reviewers serve as "lenses" to verify the quality of content posted.

History & Development

The Connexions system is available worldwide over the internet.
