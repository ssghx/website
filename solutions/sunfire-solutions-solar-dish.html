Solution Overview & Benefits

Sunfire is a solar based cooking solution.

SunFire 12:

  • 1.2 metre in diameter
  • Assembly size 65 x 60 x 10 cm weight 10 Kg
  • Suitable for households of 4-6 and
  • Boils a Litre of water in approximately 12 Minutes
  • Power output approx 600 Watts

SunFire 15:

  • 1.5 metre in diameter
  • Assembly size 80 x 72 x 10 cm weight 16 Kg
  • Suitable for households of 6 – 12 and
  • Boils a Litre of water in approximately 8 Minutes
  • Power output approx 1200 Watts

SunFire 18:

  • 1.8 metre in diameter
  • Assembly size 75 x 75 x 15 cm weight 24 Kg
  • Suitable for 12 – 40 – School feeding Schemes Crèches etc (or really hungry families who want food almost immediately)
  • Boils a Litre of water in approximately 4 Minutes (as fast as an electric kettle)
  • Power output approx 2000 Watts
