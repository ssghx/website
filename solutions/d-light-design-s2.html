Solution Overview & Benefits

The d.light S2 provides four hours of bright light on a full day’s charge and uses highly efficient LEDs. It provides a focused light that can be oriented in any direction, making it ideal for studying or working.

A solar panel is integrated into the d.light S2 to make solar charging simple and easy. The S2 can also be charged from a USB source using the USB cable or a standard Nokia AC charger.

Features:

  • One 4 Hour Brightness Setting
  • Free 2-Year Replacement Warranty

  • 5 Year Lifetime

  • No Battery Replacements Needed
  • Integrated Solar Panel
  • Dual Solar / AC Charging
  • Full Charge from Solar/AC – 8 Hours
  • Battery Charge Level Indicator
  • Glow In the Dark On/Off Button
  • Weather, Dust and Impact Resistant
