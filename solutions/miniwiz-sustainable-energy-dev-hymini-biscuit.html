Solution Overview & Benefits

The HYmini Biscuit is a handheld battery charger and universal charger/adapter, powered by solar and wind power.

The device uses a small solar panel to capture solar energy and has a pinwheel to capture wind energy. The HYmini Biscuit charges AA batteries, which then supply power to a USB port.

When not being powered by solar or wind energy sources, the HYmini Biscuit can be plugged in and used as a regular charger by using an AC adapter.

History & Development

The HYmini Biscuit is currently in the prototype testing stage.
