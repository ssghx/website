Solution Overview & Benefits

The portable Volcano Stove collapses down to 5" and makes use of passive ventilation for highly efficient cooking. Although too heavy for backpacking, it is highly portable for car camping or travel. It can be powered by three different cooking fuels--propane, charcoal, and wood, performing efficiently in all three catagories. Available for online purchase. Wholesale pricing available.
