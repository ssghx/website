Solution Overview & Benefits

A robust solar dynamo multiband radio suitable for groups or families with dual light setting and dedicated cell phone charging. Supplied with aerial kit and cell phone charging kit there is no need for electricity, kerosene or batteries.

History & Development

Encore primary is used by UN agencies and both large and small organisations to give people access to information and light. Encore Primary is used in schools and community listening groups.
