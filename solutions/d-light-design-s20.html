Solution Overview & Benefits

The d.light S20 solar powered lantern provides up to 8 hours of 365 degree illumination on a single charge. Its design allows for dynamic applications whether hung from a ceiling for room lighting, pointed for spotlighting or carried for mobile usage.

The d.light's integrated solar panel allows for easy charging simply by placing the all-in-one unit in a well lit area. The d.light can also be charged via AC outlet, and takes 8 hours to fully charge.

Each d.light S20 comes with a two year warranty, and is built to last for at least 5 years. With an exterior that is weather and dust resistant, the d.light can be used both indoors and outdoors.
