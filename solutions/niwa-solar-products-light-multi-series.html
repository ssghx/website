NIWA Solar Light "Multi" Series

Solution Overview & Benefits

The NIWA solar light "multi" series consists of the Multi 100 Plus and the Multi 300 XL. Both units contain a solar LED light and mobile phone charger.

Series Features:

  • Mobile Phone Charging
  • up to 5x longer lifetime
  • Solar & Mobile Adapter charging
  • Environment Friendly Battery
  • Water & Dust Protected
  • Multi-Flex Portable Design
  • High Energy Efficiency
  • Comfortable Light Slider
  • USB connection
  • indoor and outdoor use

Multi 100 Plus:

  • Available in three versions: Plus (1400 mAh battery), XL (2000 mAh battery) and XXL (5000 mAh battery)
  • 100 Lumen Brightness
  • LED Light - dimmable 3x Light Modes provide 3.5-12 hours of light
  • 5x mobile phone adapters
  • 24 Month Limited Warranty
  • includes 1.5W solar panel

Multi 300 XL:

  • Available in three versions: Standard (1400 mAh battery), XL (2000 mAh battery) and XXL (5000 mAh battery)
  • 300 Lumen Brightness
  • LED Light - 5x Light Modes provide 1.5-33 hours of light
  • 5x mobile phone adapters
  • 24 Month Limited Warranty
  • includes 2W solar panel
