Solution Overview & Benefits

With the increasing demand for quick to assemble, affordable housing in developing countries and natural disaster zones, Innovative Composites International's (ICI) innovative Prefabricated Modular Housing system fills the void with a simple, structurally sound, energy efficient and sustainable building product.

A key component of ICI’s composite panels is their unique thermoplastic fibre reinforced composite skin material. This skin, owned and manufactured exclusively by ICI, forms the outer layer of the panels and offers durability and strength to these lightweight panels. Impact resistant, impervious to water and made with 100% recyclable materials, the thermoplastic fibre reinforced composite skin material gives ICI’s Structure-Lite panels their durability and versatility. The thermoplastic skin can also be formed into various shapes.

The Structural Insulated Panel system allows for a range of structural possibilities, from simple emergency shelters to expansive family homes. The energy efficient systems are also ideal for use in a wide range of climates and conditions around the globe. These prefabricated modular homes can be assembled in hours or days as opposed to months.
