Solution Overview & Benefits

The Paddy Thresher is a tool designed for threshing crops. It can be operated by any Tractor or with a ten horsepower Electronic Motor. The Paddy Thresher does not break the rice while threshing it, nor does it leave any rice behind. A special blower is built in for cleaning the rice. The Paddy Thresher can thresh one acre per-hour. It also threshes maize and disposes of corn cubes. Users have also successfully used the Paddy Thresher to thresh millet Indian corn, lentil, toria, and seeds of lady finger.

There is also another model available which can be operated by a five horsepower Electric Motor with an 8-10 Diesel Engine.

History & Development

Akhtiar Agro King was founded by S. Sadhu Singh ji. Akhtiar Agro King manufactures Paddy Threshers, Wheat Threshers, Harambha Threshers, Stub Cutters, Straw Rippers, Seed Cleaners, Sun Flower Threshers, Multicrop Threshers, The Zero Till Seed Cum Fertilizer Drill and more. Akhtiar Agro King is supplying its machines all over India. Akhitar Agro King's most important criteria of quality is the satisfaction of its customers, both in services and quality.

In the last six decades, Akhtiar Agro King has introduced high-quality products in the Agriculture field, including all types of Paddy Threshers, Wheat Threshers, Harambha Threshers, Rotavators, Seed Drills , Laser Leveller, Bund Maker, Stub Cutter, Straw Ripper, Seed Cleaner, Sun Flower Threshers, Multicrop Thresher, Disc Harrow, and Roto Drill. The company came into existence under the creative entrepreneurial guidance of S. Sadhu Singh, and is now managed by his sons S.Akhtiar Singh and S. Dilbag Singh.

The qualities of inputs used are very carefully chosen. The designs, colors patterns comprise a blend of traditional and modern art.
