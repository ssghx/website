Solution Overview & Benefits

Labor Link is a mobile platform that gives companies real-time data from their supply chain or field operations, and gives workers and farmers a voice to report on conditions in their workplace or community. Labor Link participants call a local number and answer voice recorded questions using their telephone keypad. Responses are collected in real-time, aggregated, analyzed and returned to partners at mylaborlink.org.

History & Development

Labor Link was developed in 2010 and initially launched in Peru with 100 factory workers. Today, Labor Link operates in 7 countries and has reached 25,000+ farmers and workers worldwide. Labor Link's global headquarters are based in Oakland, CA.
