Solution Overview & Benefits

Peepoo is a single use bag that collects and sanitizes human waste. The bag is designed to that the users' hands are kept clean. Urea in the bag kills pathogens and the bag is sealed after use, making disposal easy and safe. The entire bag is biodegradable and can be used as fertilizer or buried. Unlike most sanitation solutions Peepoo requires no water to use, making it ideal for disaster recovery and locations where water service is not available.

History & Development

Peepoo was designed by Peepoople AG in Sweden.
