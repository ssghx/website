Solution Overview & Benefits

For less than $10 USD Uno 50 is Niwa’s entry-level solar light solution. An affordable, all-in-one solar lamp for energy independence.

Uno 50 offers up to 50 lumen of light in its highest light mode for 2 hours. The second setting of 25 lumen lasts for 4 hours - a brightness level which is enough for reading, cooking or lighting up a home. The Uno 50 can be used as a reading light, or can be hung from a wall or ceiling.

Features:

  • 50 Lumen Brightness
  • All-in-One Compact Design
  • LED Light - 2 Light Modes
  • up to 5x longer lifetime
  • Solar & Mobile Adapter charging
  • Environment Friendly Battery
  • Solar Inside (PV panel integrated)
  • Durable Strong Design
  • High Energy Efficiency
  • Comfortable Light Slider
  • Charging feedback LED
  • 24 Month Warranty
