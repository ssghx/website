Solution Overview & Benefits

The AFRISAN S.P.U.D. (Solar Powered Urine Diversion) toilet is a basic household sanitation system with a secondary composting process. A four week aerobic process consisting of dehydration and composting produces a safe, dry, and inoffensive compost-like material that is free of any odors and flies.

The AFRISAN S.P.U.D. toilet is solar-powered. The application is chemical free and thus environmentally friendly. The unit is self-contained, which prevents and prohibits the spillage of any human excreta and urine into surrounding areas. This contributes to healthier and more hygienic living conditions.

The system is easy to install and maintain.
