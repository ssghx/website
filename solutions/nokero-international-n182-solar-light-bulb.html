Solution Overview & Benefits

The Nokero N182 is specifically designed for aid organizations seeking to end energy poverty and provide emergency lighting to those in need. It is an extremely affordable and compact solar light bulb. It provides ten lumens of bright light for five hours each night. The N182 requires six to eight hours of charge time in direct sunlight. It is weatherproof and designed for several years of use. The rechargeable battery lasts approximately two years, and the lantern automatically switches off in bright light to save charge.

History & Development

The N-Series solar light bulbs capture sunlight and converts it into electricity. LED lights are powered by the reusable battery enclosed in the housing of the bulb.
