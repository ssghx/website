BIO-MOTO Bio-Fuel Cookstove

Solution Overview & Benefits

The BIO-MOTO is a bio-fuel cookstove developed by International Research and Development Africa (IRDA).

The stove has five-stage variable power (0.8 KW- 4.0 KW), instant ignition capabilities and is fuel spill and explosion proof. The BIO-MOTO cooker is available in 1, 2, 4, 6 and 8 burner configurations and has a 15 year manufacturer defects warranty.

BIO-MOTO can boil 1 liter of water in 5 minutes, 2.5 liters in 12 minutes and 5 liters in 25 minutes.

This cookstove uses a bio-fuel developed by IRDA. It is processed from agricultural food-crop stover (leaves and stalks of field crops) and post harvest starch crops that have been damaged or contaminated.

History & Development

IRDA has Government approval to enter the Kenyan Market and is setting up a local cookstove production facility.

The BIO-MOTO brand cookstoves and bio-fuel will become available through existing last-mile energy retailers and new community-based, micro-entrepreneur partners.
