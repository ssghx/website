Solution Overview & Benefits

The Nokia 215 is an affordable internet phone aimed at consumers in developing and emerging market countries. At USD $29 pre-tax, the Nokia 215 allows for affordable internet access, as well as a number of additional features, including:

  • 29 days of battery life on standby
  • Built in torch/flashlight
  • FM radio
  • Supports 2G networks
  • Dual SIM option
  • 0.3 Mega Pixel camera

The Nokia 215 will also come with pre-installed applications including Twitter, Facebook, Messenger and Bing Search.

History & Development

The Nokia 215 was announced in January of 2015, and distribution is scheduled for early 2015. Nokia's mobile phone business was acquired by Microsoft Corporation in September 2013.
