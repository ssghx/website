Solution Overview & Benefits

From Fenugreen.com: FreshPaper is a simple piece of paper that keeps fruits & veggies fresh for 2-4 times longer, organically. ​ ​ ​Made with edible organic ingredients, FreshPaper has a distinctive maple-like scent that signals that the paper is active.​​​ ​ FreshPaper works like refrigeration by inhibiting bacterial and fungal growth, as well as degradative enzymes. ​ Simply drop a small sheet into produce bags, containers, or refrigerator drawers — it's like a “dryer sheet for produce!” ​ ​Biodegradable, compostable, and recyclable, this award-winning innovation has received international recognition for its potential to change how the world keeps its food fresh.​
