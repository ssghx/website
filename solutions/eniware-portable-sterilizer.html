Solution Overview & Benefits

The Eniware Portable Sterilizer (EPS) enables safer medical care anytime, anywhere. Using a proven, patented state-of-the-art nitrogen dioxide gas technology, the EPS sterilizes medical instruments without heat, water, power or fuel. Robust, affordable EPS units are appropriate for use in low-resource healthcare settings including disaster areas, conflict zones and hospitals and clinics in the developing world.

History & Development

Founded in 2011 by Dr. James Bernstein and Huma Malik, Eniware is committed to reducing the global incidence of surgical and healthcare-associated infection by increasing prevalence of medical instrument sterilization. Eniware capitalizes on investments made in its parent company, Noxilizer, to develop its patented nitrogen dioxide (NO2) gas sterilization technology. The NO2 technology has been validated and commercialized by Noxilizer.

Eniware has been licensed and is adapting this technology for low-resource environments.​​​ The result will be a portable, power-independent, low-cost medical instrument sterilization device that will be used in developing countries (defined as all of Africa, the Middle East, the Caribbean Islands, Latin America and South America and the developing world countries of Asia), in disaster response settings, and by militaries.​
