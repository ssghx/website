Solution Overview & Benefits

The global village shelter is an easy to assemble housing system that can be rapidly deployed after an emergency. The Shelters are available in 6 and 20 meter sizes and are shipped flat. They are made entirely of plastic and can be assembled by two people in 15-20 minutes. They have an optional wood stove and can be connected to each other to form larger structures.

History & Development

The shelters are manufatured by Global Village shelters LLC. They are shipped direct by the container load.
