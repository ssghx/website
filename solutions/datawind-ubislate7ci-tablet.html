Solution Overview & Benefits

The UbiSlate7Ci is a low-cost 7" multi-touch capacitive screen Android tablet. It comes with an embedded WiFi module. The USB port allows for expanded storage and/or use of peripherals and the Android OS allows for hundreds of thousands of educational apps. Watch videos / surf the web / complete school or office work anywhere, any time.

History & Development

DataWind gained international acclaim in 2011 when the Indian government purchased the first Aakash tablet, breaking price barriers as the lowest cost tablet in the world. DataWind has also developed a patented web compression/acceleration technology to deliver the web using less data. (costing less and making it faster). In addition to its low cost to the general public, the Ubislate 7Ci can also be purchased by schools for a reduced price of $20 USD.
