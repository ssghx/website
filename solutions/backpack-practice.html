Solution Overview & Benefits

At Backpack Practice (formerly Backpack TV), you can find education videos to help you with math, science, and other academic subjects.

History & Development

Backpack Practice was founded by Joe Wagner with the help of many dedicated and bright high school and college students. Their team has created a highly curated library of education videos organized by academic subject and detailed topic. Many of our videos are linked to topics from popular textbooks, a real bonus for finding just the right video. They have partnered with teachers and are always on the lookout for more outstanding teachers to enhance their growing library.
