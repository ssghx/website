Solution Overview & Benefits

Safi International and Abry Design in Oslo have designed the Safi e-cooker. The e-cooker represents a new generation of bioethanol stoves. The e-cooker is not only clean, safe and affordable, but also fuel-efficient and colorful, with a modern design.

Its unique features offers users the following benefits:

  • The e-cooker is delivered fully assembled.
  • It is easy to use and comes with simple instructions on how to fill and refill with bioethanol.
  • The pot supports are solid and give a perfect amount of aeration.
  • Accessible handles on the front of the e-cooker allow the user to adapt the flame, and consume an optimal amount of fuel at all times.
  • The fuel canister is filled with a sponge material that absorbs the fuel and leaves no room for spilling the fuel.
  • The fuel canister is easy to slide in and out.
  • The Safi e-cooker burns with a clear blue flame that leaves no soot.
  • The burner blends the ethanol gas perfectly with the air to reach an optimal and efficient burn.

History & Development

Safi International is a leading Norwegian bioethanol stove and fuel company that designs, develops and manufactures affordable and modern bioethanol stoves for African families. Safi distributes Safi e-cookers and Safi bioethanol through dedicated local franchisees.
