Solar Cell Phone Charger

Solution Overview & Benefits

UTL-C is a PERSONAL SOLAR USB CHARGER AND LIGHT. Durable 1.5 watt solar panel, lithium battery with life of 5-7 years, two brightness settings on the light. USB port charges most cell phones (comes with cord for charging) and other small electronics such as E-readers, long flexible gooseneck, wall mountable

History & Development

Product was designed at the Institute for Energy Efficiency at UC Santa Barbara. First product called UTL-1 is a simple, durable solar light. Product is currently sold on website as a buy one, donate one. Over 55,000 lights distributed in over 65 countries since Jan. 2011. New product is being tested in Kenya and Malawi.
